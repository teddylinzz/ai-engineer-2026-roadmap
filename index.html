Target Lab Focus Areas (2026 Profile)

OpenAI Key: Scaling & Systems

Strong emphasis on full-stack AI engineering, deep understanding of scaling laws, custom PyTorch models, GPU bottlenecks, and highly optimized serving infrastructure (Triton, vLLM). Expect systems coding and algorithmic design tests.

Anthropic Key: Safety & Precision

Deeply values alignment theory, Constitutional AI, rigorous evaluation metrics (automated prompts, Ragas), and extreme coding precision. Expect heavy algorithmic screening and questions on model jailbreaking/guardrails.

Google AI Key: Scale & Multimodality

Looks for developers with experience in massive scale. Long-context management (Gemini's 2M+ token limit), multimodal processing (video/audio processing native models), TPUs, and Jax/PyTorch integration.
